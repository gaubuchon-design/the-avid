## Roadmaps, Audits, And Parity Planning

These documents are still useful, but many are aspirational or comparative
rather than descriptions of fully shipped behavior.

- parity matrices and parity audits
- refactor plans
- modernization/program docs
- competitive analysis
- specification and usability-study documents

## ADRs

Files under [adr/](adr) are historical decision records. They capture the
reasoning at the time the decision was made and may describe older
implementation details.
